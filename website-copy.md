## What We Look For

# Our acquisition criteria.

We are actively reviewing businesses with:

- $300K–$1M in EBITDA or SDE
- $1M–$5M in enterprise value
- Strong existing management or clear transition plan
- Established customer base
- Healthy operating culture
- Opportunities to grow through marketing, sales, systems, technology, or workflow improvement
- Texas or surrounding regional presence preferred

We are especially interested in founder-owned, family-owned, and succession-driven businesses where the owner cares about what happens next.

## Our Approach

# Preserve the core. Build the next chapter.

Great businesses are built by people, not spreadsheets.

We take time to understand the team, the customers, the culture, and the operating rhythm before making changes. From there, we focus on practical improvements that help the business grow without losing what made it successful in the first place.

Our growth focus includes:

- Sales and marketing outreach
- Customer retention and referrals
- KPI reporting and visibility
- Workflow automation
- AI and technology adoption where useful
- Hiring, training, and process improvement
- Financial discipline and cash flow clarity
