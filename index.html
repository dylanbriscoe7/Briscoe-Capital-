Our Mission

A lasting home for the business you built.

We look for profitable companies with loyal customers, capable teams, and a culture worth keeping. The kind of business a community would miss.

We don't strip and flip. We protect what already works, support the people behind it, and invest patiently in the next chapter of growth.

Our acquisition criteria.

We are actively reviewing businesses with:

  • $300K–$1M in EBITDA or SDE →
  • $1M–$5M in enterprise value →
  • Strong existing management or clear transition plan →
  • Established customer base →
  • Healthy operating culture →
  • Room to grow through marketing, sales, or systems →
  • Texas or surrounding regional presence preferred →

We're especially drawn to founder- and family-owned businesses where the owner cares what happens next.

Our Approach

Preserve the core. Build the next chapter.
